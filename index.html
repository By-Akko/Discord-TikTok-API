Welcome to Shangri-La

Shangri-La is a unique Discord bot that integrates seamlessly with TikTok, bringing innovative features and fun interactions directly to your Discord server.

Features

Explore the exciting features of Shangri-La:

  • Customizable TikTok content feeds

How to Use

Here's how to add Shangri-La to your server and get started:

  • Step 1: Invite Shangri-La to your Discord server.
  • Step 2: Configure your preferences and settings.
  • Step 3: Start engaging with TikTok content through Shangri-La.
